Full Service Cabins

The full-service cabins at Mount Nebo State Parks have all the amenities guests need for a relaxing getaway. Although many of the cabins were built in the 1930s by the Civilian Conservation Corps, they are updated with modern amenities including satellite television, heat, and air conditioning, full kitchens with cookware and dinnerware. Linens are provided in these cabins. Outdoor seating is also provided.
